Tara's background:

Tara Kelly was born on August 4, 1988. She grew up in Franklin Square, NY, a town in Nassau County, Long Island.
As a child, Tara took interest in sports, music & film. She spent her days playing soccer & softball, but also found the time to learn electric guitar.
Tara was also fond of computers & video games. She'd spend hours of her days on her computer playing games, and also trying to learn how to customize them to her liking.
Her affinity for computers carried into college, where her friends would often ask her to troubleshoot any minor technical issues they'd face with their laptops.
It also helped her with her studies in finance, as she was required to frequently use the function features provided in Microsoft Excel.
After college, Tara decided to follow in her father's footsteps and pursue a career in the film industry. She was inducted into the Local 52 International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees, where she worked with the props & set dressing departments. She enjoyed a successful career in the film business.
